Robotic Control

Hadron Prime™: Grant Life & Mind to Your Robots

Grant your robots intelligence & insight powered by Large Language Models. Hadron Prime allows you to build topic-based nodes for your controllers and orchestrate them with ease.

Communicate

Your nodes can communicate with others in the environment and with the other Hadron Prime nodes to share important measurements, information and insights within their own scope.

Measure

Your nodes can take in sensory data and measurements based on the environment to take actions based on the received measurements, prompts you design, and the state of others.

Process

The nodes process all the data they receive from various resources, and decide the next actions to take based on the measurements, the current state of the environment and others, and your pre-defined preferences.

Act

The nodes take actions based on the possible action space, and take the power of Large Language Models to interpret the environment and take the best action based on the current circumstances.

Swarm Intelligence

Binexus™: Self-Replicating Swarm Intelligence Network

A powerful swarm intelligence network to enable parallelizing workflows, and create a lean but scaled army of assistants capable of shaping themselves to concurrently manage multiple tasks.

Divide

The network divides your tasks into smaller atoms of more basic nature and packages them to be handled by the workers in the network.

Replicate

A group of stem nodes replicate themselves to create a swarm of worker nodes to handle the tasks in parallel and in a distributed manner.

Conquer

The swarm of worker nodes complete the individual atoms of the tasks, and self-organize to merge the resulting outputs and present them to the user.
