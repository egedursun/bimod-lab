Troubleshooting
Learn more about troubleshooting

If your assistant is not responding, check your internet connection and ensure that the assistant is active. If the issue persists, try restarting the assistant or reconfiguring its settings. Sometimes, the issue might arise from a technical glitch on our end, and if your assistant is still not responding after trying the above steps, please contact our support team for further assistance.

Inaccurate or incomplete responses may result from improperly configured settings. Adjust the assistant’s hyperparameters like temperature or max tokens, or update the assistant’s knowledge base to improve accuracy. If the issue persists, please contact our support team for further assistance and guidance for optimizing your assistant’s performance.

Ensure that the SQL database credentials are correctly entered and that the database is accessible from the network. Check the assistant's permissions and make sure it has the necessary access rights to perform I/O operations. For instance, if your database connection is marked as read-only, the assistant will not be able to write to the database. If the issue persists, please contact our support team for further assistance.

Verify that the SSH credentials and connection details are correct. Ensure the target server is accessible, and the assistant has permission to perform operations. Additionally, check if the SSH keys are properly configured. If the issue persists, please contact our support team for further assistance.
