Account Administration & Management

Organizations

Organizations are the primary wrapper for all of your BimodLab resources. You can use organizations to group resources and manage access control in a way that is easy to scale.

Organizations are also used to manage billing and payments for your BimodLab account.

Create a New Organization Manage Existing Organizations
LLM Models

Large Language Model (LLM) Cores are the connections you need to establish with your provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) to use their models as the core processor of your agents.

Each model is connected to a specific organization, and can be connected to multiple assistants in your account. To connect an LLM model you will need an API key, which can be found in the provider's platform, such as OpenAI platform.

Create LLM Model Manage LLM Models
Tracking Transactions

You can track all of your transactions in the platform, with each of the billable actions explicitly shown in the transaction history.

You can also view the details of each transaction, such as the date, time, amount, and the action that was performed by automations, assistants, you as a user, or other users in your organization.

See Transactions
Add & Transfer Balance

You can add balance to your organization to pay for the billable actions that are performed by automations, assistants, you as a user, or other users in your organization.

BimodLab is a pay-as-you-go platform, so you only pay for what you use. You can add balance to any of your organizations or transfer balance between organizations by using your debit or credit card.

Add Balance Transfer Balance

Projects and teams also enable you to use project management tools on BimodLab such as MetaKanban AI automated kanban boards, meeting tracking, team performance and tempo tracking and much more.

Managing Your Cloud Information

VoidForger

VoidForger is the meta-orchestrator AI assistant that can control all of your agents, AI teams, and automations in the platform. You can use VoidForger to create automated runtime cycles to control your enterprise workflows, and even create custom triggers and schedules for your agents.

VoidForger agent can be modified depending on your needs and can be initiated or paused at any time. You can also modify additional parameters such as intervals between cycles, and the maximum number of cycles to be executed.

Customize VoidForger
Workspace Hub

Workspace Hub is the central hub for communicating with all of your AI agents, AI teams, as well as orchestration workflows via the intermediation of VoidForger. When you are chatting with VoidForger, it can handle the communication with all of your agents and teams in the background and provide you with the results, saving you time and effort.

While workspace is a very quick hub for communication, that doesn't mean that you can't reach out to your agents individually. You can check the chats section below to see how you can communicate with your agents directly.

Managing Users, Roles & Permissions

Users

You can have multiple users under one or more organizations depending on your account setup and individual needs. Users can be connected or disconnected from organizations, and must be invited by administrator accounts to reach to the resources.

Inviting users does not automatically grant the required permissions to them to use the application. You can check the permissions and roles section to discover how you can grant permissions to users to utilize the resources in the platform.

Invite New Users Add Users to Organization Manage Users
Permissions

Permissions are the rules that determine what users can and cannot do in the platform. You can assign permissions to users by either adding them to a role, or by assigning them individual permissions.

Permissions are atomic and can be assigned to users individually, and this allows them to perform specific actions in the platform such as creating agents, managing teams, and more.

Add Permissions to Users Manage User Permissions
Roles

Unlike permissions, roles are a collection of permissions that can be assigned to users in bulk. You can create roles and assign them to users to grant them a set of permissions that are required for their job.

BimodLab also offers boilerplate roles that you can use to quickly assign permissions to users depending on your needs. You can also create custom roles and assign them to users to grant them specific permissions.

Add a Role Manage Roles Connect Roles to Users

BimodLab Complex Assistants

Complex Assistants

BimodLab custom complex assistants are the AI assistants that are designed to perform complex tasks that require multiple steps and interactions with multiple systems, data sources, tools, and services. They are the primary worker units in the platform for automating your enterprise workflows.

Although they are very powerful, they are even more powerful when they are orchestrated by agents that are higher in the hierarchy such as LeanMod assistants (Oracles), orchestrators and the VoidForger. This also helps complex assistants to be use only when needed and helps optimizing the costs as well.

Create BimodLab Assistant Manage BimodLab Assistants
Plug & Play Assistants

Creating custom complex assistants can be time-consuming and requires a bit of manual effort on your end. This is to give you all the necessary tools and freedom to truly build what you exactly need.

However, if you are looking for a quick solution, you can use the plug & play assistants that are readily available on our platform and optimized for various use cases. You can use them as they are or later modify them to fit your needs.

Discover Plug & Play Assistants
Plug & Play AI Teams

AI teams are the groups of agents that are designed to work together to achieve a common goal. They consist of worker assistants which are BimodLab complex assistants and form an expert network together, a LeanMod oracle assistants that works as a manager for them, and an orchestration manager (Maestro) that works as a coordinator for them when they work for a complex task.

Including these in the architecture, AI teams are also invariably connected to the VoidForger to be controlled and managed by it since VoidForger is the ultimate meta-orchestrator of your enterprise and workflows.

Discover AI Teams

LeanMod Lightweight & Oracle Assistants

LeanMod Assistants

LeanMod assistants are the lightweight assistants that are designed to be concise and efficient in operations rather than having the complex set of data sources and tools attached. They are more cost-effective, but at the same time quite powerful since they have the ability to reach to the BimodLab complex assistants when needed.

LeanMod assistants also work as Oracles, which are managers of AI teams and expert networks in the platform. They can use expert networks as a source of knowledge and can initiate workflows by ordering the AI teams to perform tasks.

Create LeanMod Assistant Manage LeanMod Assistants
Expert Networks

Expert networks are the groups of agents that are designed to work together to achieve a common goal. Each of these assistants can have their own data sources and tools attached to them. LeanMod assistants can consult these expert networks to get the required knowledge and information to perform their tasks.

Expert networks are the primary body of knowledge for the LeanMod assistants, and they are referenced by the LeanMod assistants if they need access to a broader knowledge base.

Create Expert Network Manage Expert Networks
Semantor Network

Although Expert Networks are the primary source of knowledge for LeanMod assistants, it is not the only one. LeanMod assistants can also consult the Semantor network to search for assistants of relevant expertise given a specific task, and then establish a connection with them to get the required knowledge.

In addition, Semantor network can also access the pre-built boilerplate assistants within BimodLab platform to get the job done, even if your account doesn't have a direct assistant integration to deal with the task at hand. You can think of Semantor network as an efficient outsourcing tool; it is powerful, yet it is not as good as in-house assistants, therefore it is a secondary source of knowledge for LeanMod assistants.

Configure Semantor

Securing Enterprise Data: NER & BeamGuard

Named Entity Recognition (NER)

Named Entity Recognition (NER) is the process of identifying and classifying named entities in text into predefined categories such as the names of persons, organizations, locations, expressions of times, quantities, monetary values, percentages, etc.

By allowing your assistants to utilize these technique, you can prevent your sensitive internal information from being delivered to your LLM providers or other third-party services. Instead, your data will be anonymized and only the necessary information will be shared with the third parties.

Create NER Integration Manage NER Integrations
BeamGuard Watch

BeamGuard is a specialized assistant that is designed to monitor the transactions between your assistants and your data sources such as SQL or NoSQL databases, as well as your server connections.

When enabled, BeamGuard will identify any risky modifications or deletions that are made by your assistants, and will ask for your manual confirmation before the action is taken.

Manage BeamGuard Artifacts

Orchestrator is also able to plan out the roadmap to achieve the goal, and can be automated and/or triggered via other assistants whenever needed.

eLLMa Scripting

eLLMa is a meta-level programming language that is designed to save time by letting the programmers focus on the architecture of a system rather than the lower-level logic of the code.

eLLMa uses a simple syntax that is easy to learn and understand, and the scripts written with eLLMa can be converted to the most of the popular programming languages with ease.

BimodLab offers a dedicated environment for you to generate, optimize and deploy smart contracts by using powerful AI assistants that can generate Web3.0 code in Solidity, and deploy them to the blockchain networks with your permission.

Hadron Prime

Hadron Prime is a specialized framework to allow robotic devices as well as IoT systems to be controlled and communicated via the BimodLab platform. You can use Hadron Prime to automate robotic control systems, and to query your devices in natural language to understand their status or actions as well as interpreting historical data.

Hadron Prime also allows multi-agent communication between different robotic devices / IoT units by connecting their contexts via messaging topics, and create a more ubiquitous environment for your devices to work together.

Create Hadron System Manage Hadron Systems
Binexus

Binexus is a framework optimized specifically for optimizing prompts to evolve the best agent for performing a certain task. It works by using genetic and evolutionary algorithms to use rules of natural selection, recombinations, and mutations to evolve the best agent for the task.

Binexus can be used to optimize the agents for a wide range of tasks, from simple tasks to more complex tasks, and also allows for defining custom genes to be optimized for different use cases.

You can also customize how your agents interpret information on web, determine their selectiveness for the information, set whitelists or blacklists to prevent them from accessing certain websites, and even determine whether they have access to page source and scripts.

Servers & SSH

You can connect your assistants to your servers by using SSH connections, and manage your server with the power of AI assistants. You can let your assistants read your folder structure, internal data and configurations, or you can also allow them to modify the data on your server and execute commands on your behalf.

Your assistants will also be able to write bash scripts or use the ones you have defined, to perform certain actions on your behalf in your server environment. Don't worry, you can always monitor the actions of your assistants and prevent them from doing any harmful actions thanks to the BeamGuard.

SQL Databases

You can connect your SQL databases to your assistants to let them read and write data to your databases, depending on your configuration. If you prevent them from changing data, they will only be able to read the data from your databases, otherwise, they can perform modifications or deletions on your behalf.

You can also allow your assistants to execute custom SQL queries on your databases, or let them use queries that you personally define as well. Similar to SSH servers, these transactions will be monitored by BeamGuard to prevent any harmful actions.
