Models¶

This section describes the models used in the Community Forum app.

This module defines the data models for a community forum application. The models include ForumCategory, ForumThread, ForumPost, ForumComment, and ForumLike, which represent different aspects of the forum such as categories, threads, posts, comments, and likes respectively.
